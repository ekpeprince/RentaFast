# RentaFast Cross River

RentaFast is a modern, AI-powered property marketplace specifically designed for the Cross River State rental market. We connect verified landlords and agents in Calabar, Ikom, Ogoja, and Obudu with serious tenants through a seamless, trust-based platform.

## Key Features

- **AI Home Matcher**: Tenants describe their ideal home in plain English, and our AI analyzes listings to find the perfect fit.
- **Neighborhood Vibe Checks**: Localized AI guides that give tenants a "Vibe Check" on Cross River neighborhoods (e.g., proximity to UNICAL, nightlife in Marian, or serenity in Obudu).
- **Verified Agent Badge**: Landlords can upload verification documents to earn a trust badge, increasing their conversion rates.
- **Smart Listing Generator**: Landlords use "Magic Write" to generate professional, catchy property descriptions based on simple bullet points.
- **Leads Dashboard**: Real-time performance tracking for landlords, including view counts, favorite counts, and formal application management.
- **Real-time Messaging**: Direct, secure communication between tenants and agents.

## Tech Stack

- **Framework**: Next.js 15 (App Router)
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS + ShadCN UI
- **Database & Auth**: Firebase (Firestore & Authentication)
- **Generative AI**: Genkit + Gemini 2.0 Flash
- **Icons**: Lucide React

## For Landlords & Agents

1. **Sign Up**: Choose the 'Landlord' role.
2. **Get Verified**: Upload your ID or property documents in your account settings to earn the "Verified Agent" badge.
3. **List Home**: Use the "List Home" button. Don't forget to use **Magic Write** to make your description stand out.
4. **Manage Leads**: Track your property performance and review tenant resumes in the Leads Dashboard.

## For Tenants

1. **Sign Up**: Choose the 'Tenant' role.
2. **Build Your Resume**: Complete your profile with your occupation and bio. This is your "Renter Resume" that landlords see.
3. **Set Your Wishlist**: Use the **Smart Matcher** to tell the AI exactly what you need.
4. **Explore**: Use the **Vibe Guides** to find the neighborhood that matches your lifestyle.
5. **Apply**: Click "Rent Now" to send a formal application and start a chat with the agent.
